How ExportLots
works for you

Three ways to partner with Dapex — each with its own process, access level and earning model. Choose the one that fits your business.

01 — Wholesale Dealer

You buy cars.
We ship them.

You're a dealer or lot owner in your country moving 3+ cars per month. You pick the cars from our live catalog — we handle everything from auction purchase to port delivery.

1
Apply & get approved
Fill out the dealer application. Our owner reviews every application manually — usually within 24 hours. Once approved, you get full access to the live catalog with real prices and countdown timers.
Manual review — 24h typical
2
Browse live inventory
Access 100–300 live lots from US stock — Copart, IAAI, and other major auctions. Every listing shows real countdown to auction end, grade, mileage, condition report, and all-in price to your specific port — no hidden fees.
VIN protected — prevents bypassing
3
Pick from our catalog — or bring your own car
Choose a vehicle from our live inventory and we buy it for you. Or — source the car yourself from any US dealer, auction, or private seller. Just tell us the pickup address and we handle everything from there. You are not required to use our inventory.
We pick up from any US location
4
We handle pickup, docs & loading
Whether we bought the car or you did — we dispatch inland truckers, transport to our NJ facility, and handle all export documentation in-house: AES, ISF, and our own HBL. We are a licensed NVOCC, not an agent.
FMC NVOCC #022641 — we issue our own HBL
5
Loaded into weekly container
Cars are loaded into 40HC containers at our NJ warehouse. We cut containers every week — no waiting months for a full load. You get the booking confirmation and container number.
Weekly container cuts from NJ
6
Delivered to your port
Ocean freight to Lagos, Jebel Ali, Tema, Klaipeda, Rio Haina, Poti, or Yerevan. You receive original US title, Bill of Lading, and commercial invoice. Your customs broker handles clearance on arrival.
7 ports — weekly sailings
What you pay
Our service fee $200/car
Auction purchase price At cost
Ocean freight + AES/ISF/HBL At cost
Inland transport (NJ) At cost

All-in price shown per car in the catalog. No surprises at loading.

Requirements
Minimum volume 3+ cars/month
Business type Dealer or lot owner
Approval Manual — 24h

02 — Web Portal Owner

You show our cars.
We handle everything else.

You run a car listing website in your country — or want to build one. Display our live US inventory on your platform. When your users buy, you earn commission. No inventory risk, no shipping headaches.

1
Apply as a portal partner
Submit your application with details about your platform — website, audience, country, and estimated monthly volume. Our owner reviews and approves manually.
Manual review — 24–48h typical
2
Get access to our inventory feed
Once approved, you receive dealer-level access to the full catalog. Display our listings on your site — live countdowns, photos, grades, and market-specific prices. Your users see your brand.
API access discussed on approval
3
Your client orders through you
A retail buyer or dealer on your platform wants a car. They contact you. You pass the order to us on WhatsApp — we handle purchase, export, and shipping exactly as with direct dealers.
You stay the face to your clients
4
We ship, you track
Your clients can track their shipment through our system. You have visibility on all orders placed through your portal — container number, ETD, ETA, document status.
Tracking access for your clients
5
Earn commission per transaction
Your commission is calculated per car shipped through your portal, depending on client type. Retail buyers earn you the most. Dealer referrals pay a passive residual forever.
Paid per container departure
What you earn
Retail client buys through your portal up to $350/car
Your dealer client buys through us $150/car
You refer a wholesale dealer $50/car passive

Commission paid per car at container departure. Exact rate depends on volume and client mix — discussed on approval.

Requirements
Platform Active website
Market One of our 7 routes
Approval Manual — 24–48h

03 — Reseller

Know dealers?
Earn for every car they ship.

You're connected in your local auto market — dealers, lot owners, importers. Refer them to ExportLots and earn $50 per car they ship with us, forever. No work after the introduction.

1
Apply as a reseller
Fill out the short application. Tell us your country and how you know dealers in your market. Approval is typically same day.
Usually approved same day
2
Get your referral link
You receive a unique referral link to share with dealers. When a dealer registers and gets approved through your link, they're permanently linked to your account.
Unique link — permanent attribution
3
Introduce dealers to ExportLots
Share your link with dealers you know. That's your only job. We handle their onboarding, catalog access, purchases, and shipping. You don't need to manage anything.
Zero ongoing work required
4
Earn $50 on every car they ship
Every time your referred dealer ships a car through us — today, next month, next year — you earn $50. There's no expiry on the referral. The more active dealers you refer, the more passive income you build.
$50/car — no expiry, forever
5
3 active referrals → upgrade to dealer
Bring in 3 dealers who are actively shipping with us and you automatically qualify for wholesale dealer status — giving you direct access to buy cars at dealer pricing yourself.
Auto-upgrade at 3 active referrals
What you earn
Per car shipped by your referral $50/car
Referral expiry None — forever
Referral limit Unlimited

Example: 5 dealers each shipping 10 cars/month = $2,500/month passive income.

Requirements
Investment Zero
Ongoing work Zero
Upgrade path 3 active refs → dealer

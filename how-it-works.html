Common questions
Is the $500 really refundable?
Yes — fully. If we can't secure the exact car you reserved within 14 days, we return the full $500. No fees, no deductions. You can also choose to have us search for a similar car instead.
Can I get financing for the rest of the payment?
Yes. Your local dealer partner can offer financing on the remaining balance. Terms depend on your country and the dealer. Ask us and we'll connect you with the right dealer in your area.
What documents do I receive?
Original US title, Bill of Lading, commercial invoice, and all export documents required for customs clearance in your country. Everything is prepared by our team at Dapex — FMC NVOCC #022641.
How long does shipping take?
We ship weekly from Newark, NJ. Ocean transit is approximately 21-28 days to Nigeria and Ghana, 25-30 days to UAE, 16-20 days to Lithuania, 8-12 days to Dominican Republic, and 25-30 days to Georgia/Armenia.
What if the car arrives damaged?
All cars are insured during transit. Any damage that occurs during loading or shipping is covered. We recommend adding marine insurance — available for $150 per car.
Which countries do you ship to?
Nigeria (Apapa), Ghana (Tema), UAE (Jebel Ali), Lithuania (Klaipeda), Dominican Republic (Rio Haina), Georgia (Poti), and Armenia (Yerevan via Poti). More destinations coming soon.
